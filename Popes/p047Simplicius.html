St. Simplicius


Successor 🠖 St. Felix II

St. Simplicius (468-483) endorsed the opposition of Leo I to the twenty-eighth Article of the Council of Chalcedon; and opposed the emperor's plan to make the Monophysite, Peter Mongos, Bishop of Alexandria. He also confirmed the condemnation of the Monophysites and rejected the Henoticon>.
